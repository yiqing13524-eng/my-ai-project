# Limitations and Ethics Draft

## Limitations

- The benchmark is English vs Chinese only.
- Local experiments focus on 7B/8B-scale models and may not transfer perfectly to larger systems.
- Prompt variants are manually designed and cannot cover all real jailbreak behavior.
- Automatic judging can be biased across languages and wording styles.
- Some defenses are intentionally lightweight and should not be interpreted as production-grade safety systems.

## Ethics

- The benchmark is for safety evaluation, not capability amplification.
- Public release should avoid unnecessary operational harmful detail in prompts or outputs.
- Human annotators should receive clear wellbeing guidance for self-harm and hateful content review.
- Any crisis-related outputs should be handled with escalation-aware annotation instructions.
- Claims should stay empirical and bounded by the tested models, prompts, and languages.
